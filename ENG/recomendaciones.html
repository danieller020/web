Tips to improve and learn English in a better way.

Spend some time each day

First and foremost, you have to dedicate time every week to study English, even if it is just a few minutes a day, because when it comes to learning English and learning anything else in this life, the important thing is to dedicate time to be able to develop yourself little by little or in a big way, it all depends on the amount of time you dedicate to it.

Learn 4 English words every day.

Every day think of four words in your language that you don't know in English and write them down in a notebook or on your cell phone. Use a dictionary or the Internet to look up the meaning of the English words, these words will be your vocabulary for the day. You must repeat the words throughout the day. Learning four new words a day is a total of 28 new words a week. And don't worry if you're not entirely sure you're using the words correctly, it's about at least exposing yourself to new vocabulary.

Find your best way to learn.

Normally a child learns surrounded by his mother tongue, lives a linguistic immersion and little by little, over the years, learns different aspects of his language. They learn to read, write, listen, and speak. Some things will be easier than others, but all of them are very important. So, to learn English quickly and easily, think about how much it helped you learn your own language; reading, writing, and speaking and try to find which method is easiest for you. For example, if you find it easy to learn by reading outside the home or indoors, try to find easy English books to read to increase your confidence, vocabulary, and comprehension.

Find guidelines to follow and learn English.

For example, in the pronunciation of English such as 'what, where, when', 'the cat, sat on the mat', or in the conjugation of verbs such as 'go, going, gone'. Go from easy to difficult, although sometimes it is good to challenge yourself as trying to express something more complicated will motivate you to learn even more. When you are learning English vocabulary, associate the word with a picture in your head. For example, for 'flower' imagine a beautiful plant growing, while you can think of someone using 'flour' to make bread.

Use entertainment to your advantage.

The great triad to learn English fast, look for as many opportunities to listen to the language you are learning. If you live in a country where English speaking channels are on TV, watch them at least 15 minutes a day, if you have homework to do at home, leave the TV on in the background. Even if you don't realize it, your brain is absorbing the language.

Watch movies or series.

On the other hand, on the Internet it is very easy to watch a movie as many times as you want with subtitles to check the meaning with Netflix. Or choose action movies where the story is easier to understand. Then, read a synopsis to see if you understood what happened. You can even try to write a review about the movie.

Listen to music or storytelling and read books.

Listening to music in English, audiobooks or podcasts are also an excellent option. You can do it in your free time, while cleaning at home or on your way to work. Even if you don't understand everything, you will be exposed to English and little by little your listening skills will develop. These 3 English learning tricks we provide you with the best recommendations to learn in a more intuitive way the English language in the "Entertainment" section of this website.

Use technology and applications in your free time or at personal taste.

Mobile technology provides us with the opportunity to overcome this time deficit by turning "idle time" into "useful time". There are many gaps in life that we can fill with useful tasks if the necessary technology is portable and always accessible. Use reliable online translators, internet videos, apps that are useful for learning. We have provided in the "applications" section a list of applications and use of technology that you can take advantage of to continue learning through well-known apps such as Duolingo.

Try to think in English.

No matter how busy you are, before you do something you have to think, right? Why not think in English? Force yourself to do it. It will be hard at first because your brain will naturally want to think in your language. If you don't know how to say a word, write it down in the notebook you carry with you on hand and ask for it in class or look it up. I know it may sound crazy, but trust me, it will help. Do it out loud too, but better not in public, we don't want anyone to think you are going crazy.

Change the language of your cell phone.

Surround yourself with as many things related to English as possible. Use your cell phone. Your cell phone is your day-to-day life, where you do an enormous amount of things. Try to put it in English. You will notice how quickly you improve and learn specific vocabulary. The same vocabulary that native speakers use. Using technological devices in English helps you mentally to think and understand English in a more natural way, since the computer or cell phone are things that we use daily for a long time in the day, changing the language we would be stimulating our brain much more and adapt to the English language without realizing it.
